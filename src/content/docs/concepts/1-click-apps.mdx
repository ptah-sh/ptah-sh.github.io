1-Click Apps are preconfigured applications that can be quickly deployed to your server node in Ptah.sh. They provide a streamlined way to set up common services or processes without the need for manual configuration.

## Benefits of 1-Click Apps

- **Ease of Use**: Deploy complex applications with a single click.
- **Time-Saving**: Avoid manual setup and configuration processes.
- **Standardization**: Ensure consistent deployment across your infrastructure.
- **Best Practices**: Apps are preconfigured following industry best practices.

## Using 1-Click Apps

1. Navigate to the service creation page in your Ptah.sh dashboard.
2. Browse the available 1-Click Apps templates.
3. Select the app template you want to use for your service.
4. Click "Use template" to apply the 1-Click App configuration to your new service.
5. Review and adjust any configurable options if needed.
6. Complete the service creation process.

This method allows you to quickly set up a new service based on a pre-configured 1-Click App template, saving time and ensuring best practices are followed.

## Customization

After applying a 1-Click App template to your service, you can still customize it to better suit your specific needs. This might include:

- Adjusting environment variables
- Modifying configuration files
- Scaling resources
- Adding or removing specific components

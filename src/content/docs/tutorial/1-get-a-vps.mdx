## Choosing a Hosting Provider

When setting up your server, you have several options for hosting providers. Here, we'll discuss two popular choices: Hetzner and DigitalOcean.

<Aside type="note" title="VPS Requirements">
  The specific requirements for your VPS are:
  - x86_64 hardware architecture
  - Ubuntu 24.04 operating system
  - Minimum 1 GiB RAM
  - Minimum 20 GiB disk space
</Aside>

### Hetzner

#### Why Choose Hetzner?

Hetzner is an excellent choice for hosting your server for several reasons:

1. **Competitive Pricing**: Hetzner offers some of the most cost-effective solutions in the market, providing great value for your money.

2. **Performance**: Their servers are known for their robust performance and reliability.

3. **Data Centers in Europe**: If your target audience is primarily in Europe, Hetzner's data centers provide low-latency connections.

4. **Scalability**: Hetzner offers a wide range of server options, from small VPS instances to dedicated servers, allowing you to scale as your needs grow.

5. **Good Support**: While primarily self-service, Hetzner provides solid technical support when needed.

### DigitalOcean

#### Why Choose DigitalOcean?

DigitalOcean is another popular option for hosting, with its own set of advantages:

1. **User-Friendly Interface**: DigitalOcean is known for its simple, intuitive control panel, making it easy for beginners to get started.

2. **Global Data Centers**: With multiple data centers around the world, DigitalOcean allows you to host your server closer to your target audience.

3. **Extensive Documentation**: DigitalOcean provides a wealth of tutorials and documentation, which is especially helpful for those new to server management.

4. **Developer-Friendly Features**: With features like team accounts and API access, DigitalOcean caters well to development teams.

5. **Flexible Pricing**: Their pricing model is straightforward and allows you to pay by the hour, which can be cost-effective for temporary or experimental projects.
